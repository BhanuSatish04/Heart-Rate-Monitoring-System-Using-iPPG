Instructions & Information

For Best Results:

  • Sit still and face the camera directly
  • Ensure good, even lighting on your face
  • Remove glasses if possible
  • Stay within 2-3 feet of the camera
  • Minimize movement during measurement

How iPPG Works:

Imaging photoplethysmography detects subtle color changes in your skin caused by blood flow. The camera captures these minute variations, which are then processed to extract your heart rate.
